Zine Design

This project was for my Intro to Graphic Design class. The assignment was to create a zine inspired by an object I was assigned at the beginning of the class. The object I received for this project was steel wool. I began my process by creating a list of topics and qualities relating to steel wool. I decided to create a narrative based around the tangled and knotted nature of steel wool. For me personally, this zine is about self-esteem and self-love, with the wire over each image representing a person’s inner thoughts and mindset. The wire starts out as a tangled knot and gradually becomes untangled throughout the zine. However, I wanted to keep the narrative vague enough that each viewer could find their own personal meaning in it. During critiques, it was cool to hear each person’s ideas of what this zine meant to them.

Covid-19 Poster Design

This project was for my Intro to Graphic Design class. The assignment was to make a Social Action Poster. I decided to make my poster about the unsung heroes of the Covid-19 pandemic. Throughout the pandemic, there has been a movement encouraging people to recognize and thank sanitary workers, delivery drivers, cashiers, healthcare staff, etc. I wanted my poster to further this movement, and also encourage people to continue to respect these everyday heroes even after the pandemic has ended. I chose to create a black and white poster, but left the gloved hand and rag in color to emphasize the human element of the poster.
